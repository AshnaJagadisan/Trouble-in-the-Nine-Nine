Trouble in the Nine-Nine

Adventures with the 99 crew

Introduction

Life in the 99 precinct has been running smoothly until Gina doesn't show up to work for a few days. Detectives Jake Peralta and Charles Boyle notice the absence of Gina's presence and start to get worried.

They receive an anonymous call with a deep, raspy voice that was difficult to understand. The only audible words from the caller were "Gina" and "dead".

Jake and Charles decide to take action and try to find Gina.

What should Jake and Charles do?

1. Trace the phone call 2. Go to Gina's house to look for her
