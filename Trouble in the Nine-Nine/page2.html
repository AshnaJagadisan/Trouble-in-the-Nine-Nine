Tracing the Call

Jake and Charles trace the anonymous phone call and it leads to a house in Chicago; Gina's grandmother's house. They decide to go to the house in hopes of Gina being there.

When they reach the house, Gina's grandmother opens the door and immediately faints as soon as she catches a glimpse of Charles' face. She is rushed off to the hospital in an ambulance.

Jake and Charles search her house however, Gina is nowhere to be seen. No new leads were gathered.
